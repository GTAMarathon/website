As a year of many firsts for the event, GTAM is turning hybrid!

Join us at the Eldon Building of The University of Portsmouth, England, April 7 to the 11th for a truly first of its kind and personal GTAM experience! If you choose to follow us from the comfort of your home like previously, you can find us, as always, at our Twitch channel.

This event also marks the first time that we get together for charity! We are honored to announce that GTAM2025 is benefitting War Child, a charitable foundation aiming to keep children safe during times of conflict.

You can join the GTAM community ahead of the marathon at our official Discord server, where we share the most recent updates about the event, so that you never miss a beat!

Make sure to follow us on our other socials too, like our YouTube, X (formerly Twitter), and at long last on our Bluesky!
